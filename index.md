## FAQ

**How do I download language packs?**
Go to Settings and tap on a language to download its translation pack. Packs are stored on your device for offline use.

**How do I change my base language?**
Go to Settings and select a new base language. You may need to download new language packs for the updated pairing.

**How do I delete my account?**
Contact us at shhlate.support@gmail.com and we will process your request.
